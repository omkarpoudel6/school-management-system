Introduction

We are one of the fastest growning school in nepal. The motto of our school is Education For Everyone. The main idea behind the establishment of this school is to provide quality education to those children whose family economic status is below average. There are 7 branches of our school across Nepal so that every student in every corner of our country can get quality education.

Also known as Samata boarding school.
